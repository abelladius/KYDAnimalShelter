Our Shelter

At KYD Animal Shelter, we're more than just a shelter; we're a safe haven for Uganda's stray dogs. Nestled in the heart of the beautiful Ugandan countryside, our shelter provides a loving environment where dogs find solace, comfort, and a second chance at life.

Our dedicated team of veterinarians, caregivers, and volunteers work tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome our four-legged friends. We believe in the power of love and compassion, and every success story warms our hearts.

Our shelter is not just a place; it's a community of like-minded animal lovers, local partnerships, and global supporters who share our vision of a better world for these loyal companions.
