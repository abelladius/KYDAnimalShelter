Welcome to KYD Animal Shelter

Where Every Paw Tells a Story of Resilience and Hope

At KYD Animal Shelter, we believe that every life matters, especially those who can't speak for themselves. We are dedicated to providing love, care, and hope to stray and wounded dogs in Uganda.

What we offer to our doggos?

Rescue & Care

We rescue and provide loving care to injured and abandoned dogs.

Love & Compassion

Donate & Support

Your generous donations help us continue our mission and save lives.
