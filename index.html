Welcome to KYD Animal Shelter

Where Every Paw Tells a Story of Resilience and Hope

At KYD Animal Shelter, we believe that every life matters, especially those who can't speak for themselves. We are dedicated to providing love, care, and hope to stray and wounded dogs in Uganda.

What we offer to our doggos?

Rescue & Care

We rescue and provide loving care to injured and abandoned dogs.

Love & Compassion

We believe in the power of love and compassion in healing hearts.

Donate & Support

Your generous donations help us continue our mission and save lives.

Most importantly, we care!

Nestled in the heart of Uganda, where the first light of each day reveals tales of resilience, we proudly stand as guardians of hope. Above all, we care! Our commitment is rooted in the profound belief that every life, no matter how small, deserves love and compassion. As we navigate the intricate tapestry of Uganda's landscapes, we are fueled by the unwavering dedication to make a positive impact. Through the collaborative efforts of compassionate hearts, we strive to create a haven for those who cannot speak for themselves. Your support becomes the bridge to brighter tomorrows for these sentient beings. Join us on this transformative journey, where caring isn't just a word; it's a promise to uplift, protect, and cherish every soul that crosses our path.
